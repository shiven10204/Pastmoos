About Pastmoos

Our story

Welcome to Pastmoos! We are a small but passionate team of designers and creatives who love to create beautiful and unique stickers, posters, and t-shirts. Our goal is to provide our customers with high-quality products that they will love and enjoy for years to come.

Our Products

At Pastmoos, we believe that every product should tell a story. That's why we put a lot of thought and care into each and every design we create. From our quirky and fun stickers to our thought-provoking posters, our products are designed to inspire, amuse, and delight our customers.

Our Philosophy

At Pastmoos, we believe in the power of creativity to make the world a better place. We believe that everyone has the ability to be creative, and we strive to inspire creativity in others through our products and our brand. We also believe in the importance of sustainability, and we are committed to using eco-friendly materials and practices whenever possible.

Meet Our Team

Our team is made up of talented and passionate individuals who are dedicated to creating beautiful and unique products. We are a small team, but we work closely together to bring our designs to life. We are always striving to learn and grow, both as individuals and as a company, and we are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience.
